What you get

Everything a practising advocate needs

Designed from the ground up for Indian legal practice. No bloat, no confusion — just the tools that matter in court.

Case Registry

Create and manage unlimited cases. Each case is a self-contained workspace for all your notes, files, and dates.

In-Depth Brief Points

Write detailed arguments, factual summaries, and legal points in a rich editor — always attached to the right case.

Smart Date Reminders

Mark hearings, filing deadlines, and depositions. Get notified automatically 3 days before anything critical.

Document Tracking

Log every exhibit, affidavit, or document reference per case. Never lose track of what's been filed where.

Private & Secure

Your data lives in your browser — encrypted in local storage. No third-party servers, no subscription lock-in.

Instant Access

Open your browser and you're in. No installs, no syncing delays — your practice is always one click away.

Workflow

From new case to court-ready in minutes

AdvoHQ keeps your workflow linear and friction-free. Spend your mental energy on the law, not on the software.
